Our Services

Exterior

Full exterior wash using premium soap and microfiber towels. We remove dirt, grime, and contaminants from every panel, leaving your paint clean and ready for protection.

Interior

Complete interior cleaning including vacuuming all carpets and seats, wiping down all hard surfaces, cleaning door jambs, and deodorizing the cabin for a fresh, like-new feel.

Wax & Paint Sealant

Application of premium carnauba wax or synthetic sealant to protect your paint from UV rays, environmental damage, and light scratches while adding a deep, glossy shine.

Paint Decontamination

Clay bar treatment to safely remove bonded contaminants like industrial fallout, rail dust, and tree sap that regular washing cannot eliminate. Essential before any paint protection.

Ceramic Coatings

Professional application of System X Ceramic coating — a premium nano-ceramic formula that bonds directly to your paint for years of hydrophobic protection, UV resistance, and a deep, mirror-like gloss that washes can't strip away.

Engine Bay

Careful degreasing and cleaning of the engine bay to remove grease, oil, and grime. Dressed and protected for a clean presentation and easier maintenance inspections.

Tire & Trim Care

Deep cleaning and dressing of tires and exterior plastic trim to restore a rich, dark finish. Prevents fading, cracking, and UV damage on rubber and plastic surfaces.
